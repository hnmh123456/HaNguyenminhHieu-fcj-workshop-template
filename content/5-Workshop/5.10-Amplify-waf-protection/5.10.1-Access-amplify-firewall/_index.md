#### Goal

In this step, you will open the firewall management page for your Amplify app and begin enabling AWS WAF.

#### Instructions

![Image 1](/images/5-Workshop/5.10-Amplify-waf-protection/anh1.png)

1. Sign in to the AWS Management Console and open AWS Amplify.
2. Select your application, for example `DoAnMiniGame`.
3. On the Overview page, locate `Get to production` and click `Enable firewall`.
4. If you do not see the button, open the left menu and go to `Hosting` → `Firewall`.

#### Expected result

After opening the firewall page, you are ready to proceed to the detailed WAF configuration step.
