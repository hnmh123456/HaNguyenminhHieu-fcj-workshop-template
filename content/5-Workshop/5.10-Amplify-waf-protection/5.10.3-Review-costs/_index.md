#### Goal

In this step, you will review the estimated costs and confirm the activation of WAF for your Amplify application.

#### Instructions

![Image 1](/images/5-Workshop/5.10-Amplify-waf-protection/anh3.png)

1. Scroll to the bottom of the page to review the cost estimate.
2. Take note of the charges:
   - AWS WAF fee: around $8/month plus $1.40 per 1 million requests.
   - Amplify Firewall fee: around $15/month.
3. After confirming your settings, click `Add firewall` in the lower-right corner.

#### Note

These costs may vary depending on actual traffic and the number of requests received by your application.
