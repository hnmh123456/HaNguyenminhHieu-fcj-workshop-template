## Overall Evaluation

### 1. Work Environment
The working environment at FCAJ left a very positive impression on me due to the friendliness, openness, and approachability among all members. From the very first days, I felt a sincere welcome, with no distance between official employees and interns. Whenever I encountered difficulties in my work, my colleagues were always willing to support me, even outside of official working hours, which made me feel cared for and not alone in my learning process. The workspace is arranged neatly, scientifically, and comfortably, creating favorable conditions for me to fully focus on my work, while also contributing to reducing stress during days with a heavy workload. 

*However, I think that if the company could organize more gathering and networking activities outside of work—such as team building, small parties, or informal sharing sessions—the relationships between team members would be more deeply consolidated, while helping interns like me integrate faster into the company's general culture.*

### 2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin
I felt truly fortunate to work under the guidance of a dedicated and experienced mentor. My mentor not only guided me in a detailed and specific manner through each task but was also always patient enough to explain things thoroughly whenever I did not fully understand an issue. What I appreciated most was that my mentor always encouraged me to boldly ask questions and not be afraid of making mistakes, viewing it as a natural part of the learning process. Instead of giving answers immediately, my mentor usually created opportunities for me to think, experiment, and find solutions on my own, and only then did we discuss and analyze together to draw lessons. This approach helped me train my independent thinking and problem-solving skills much more proactively than just being provided with answers.

In addition, the admin team also supported me very enthusiastically with administrative tasks, from preparing paperwork and internship contracts to answering questions related to the workflow, helping me securely and completely focus on my expertise without having to worry much about arising procedures.

### 3. Relevance of Work to Major
The tasks assigned during the internship process are closely linked to the foundational knowledge I learned at university, giving me the opportunity to apply theory to practice in a vivid and practical way. At the same time, the work also opened up completely new fields for me that I had never approached before, forcing me to actively learn and research further to complete tasks well. This harmonious combination of the old and the new not only helped me consolidate my specialized knowledge base more firmly but also equipped me with valuable practical skills—things that textbooks alone can hardly provide. As a result, I clearly felt the growth in my professional mindset after just a short internship period.

### 4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities
My internship time here was truly a valuable learning journey for me. I had the opportunity to become familiar with and more proficiently use project management tools commonly used in corporate environments, practice effective teamwork skills, as well as professional communication skills when interacting with colleagues and stakeholders. In addition to hard skills, I also learned how to manage time, prioritize tasks, and stay calm when facing deadline pressure. In particular, the sincere sharing from my mentor about practical experience and career orientation helped me have a clearer and more realistic view, thereby building a more suitable career development plan for myself in the future.

### 5. Company Culture & Team Spirit
The company culture here carries a very positive and humanistic spirit: everyone always respects one another, works with a serious and responsible attitude, but still does not forget to keep the working atmosphere fun and comfortable. Whenever there is an urgent project or an increased workload, I noticed that the team spirit is clearly demonstrated when everyone, regardless of position or rank, is ready to join hands to support each other to achieve the common goal. It was this that made me, even though only an intern, still feel that I was truly an important link, trusted, and considered an indispensable part of the collective. This is an experience that I believe not every internship environment can bring.

### 6. Policies / Benefits
In terms of policies and benefits, the company has shown significant consideration for interns by providing an internship allowance along with a flexible working hours policy, helping me balance well between studying at university and my internship. In addition, the opportunity to participate in internal training sessions is also a very big plus, because this is not only an occasion for me to supplement my specialized knowledge but also helps me expand my understanding of the company's general operations, thereby gaining a more comprehensive view of the corporate environment.
